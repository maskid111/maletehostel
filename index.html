About MaleteHostel

Learn more about our mission and services

MaleteHostel is a student-focused platform designed to make finding and securing private hostels in Malete easier, faster, and more affordable.

We understand how stressful it can be for students—especially those at Kwara State University (KWASU)—to search for accommodation that fits their budget and location needs. That's why we created MaleteHostel: a one-stop solution where students can browse verified hostel listings, compare prices, and filter options based on what truly matters.

With MaleteHostel, you can:

Search & Discover

Explore a wide range of private hostels available in Malete.

Affordable Options

Find hostels that match your budget, from shared rooms to self-contained apartments.

Filter by Needs

Narrow down choices by price, location, amenities, and room type.

Save & Collect

Bookmark your favorite hostels for easy comparison later.

Verified Listings

Every hostel listed on MaleteHostel is screened to ensure safety and reliability.

Our goal is simple: to help students find the right place to stay without stress.
